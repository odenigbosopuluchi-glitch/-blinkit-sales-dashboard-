# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

A comprehensive data analytics project transforming raw grocery transaction data into an interactive *Power BI Dashboard. This project delivers critical business insights regarding sales distribution, customer satisfaction, and inventory optimization for **Blinkit* (India's last-minute app).

---

## 📊 Key Executive Metrics
* *Total Sales Revenue:* $1.20M  
* *Average Ticket Size:* $141 per order  
* *Total Inventory Moving:* 8,523 distinct items  
* *Average Customer Rating:* 4.0 / 5.0 ⭐

## 💡 Strategic Business Insights

### 🏪 Channel Performance
* *Supermarket Type 1 Dominance:* Acts as the primary revenue engine, driving *66% of total sales ($787,529)* across all active channels while managing the largest inventory segment (5,577 items).
* *Supermarket Type 2 & 3 vs. Grocery Stores:* While Grocery Stores move a healthy volume of individual items (1,083), their lower average order value presents an optimization opportunity compared to high-margin Supermarket types.

### 📦 Consumer & Product Trends
* *Health-Conscious Demand:* Low-fat item variants heavily outperform regular alternatives, generating *$776K* in sales compared to $425K.
* *Top Revenue Categories:* Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods tie as the top-grossing categories, drawing *$0.18M* each.

### 📍 Geographic & Demographic Breakdown
* *Tier 3 Market Leadership:* High-density Tier 3 locations lead market revenue with a massive *$472.13K*, followed sequentially by Tier 2 ($393.13K) and Tier 1 ($336.29K) markets.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Analytical Techniques
* *BI Platform:* Microsoft Power BI Desktop
* *Data Transformation:* Power Query (ETL pipeline, data cleaning, and column profiling)
* *Data Modeling:* Star Schema architecture with optimized table relationships
* *Analytical Calculations:* Formulated custom DAX measures for core business KPIs (Total Sales, Average Ratings, Filter-compliant Item Visibilities)

---

## 🚀 Interactive Features
* *Filter Panel Sidebar:* Dynamic slicing by Outlet Location Type, Outlet Size, and Item Type.
* *Trend Over Time:* Visual tracking of outlet establishment performance spanning from 2011 to 2022.
